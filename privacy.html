Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 25, 2026

Overview

NODE respects your privacy. We only collect information that is necessary to make the product work, improve the experience, and support community features like the Forum and Project Hub.

We do not sell personal data.

What we collect

When you use NODE or our website, we may collect basic information such as:

  • Account information (like name or email, if you create an account)
  • Usage data (like pages visited or features used)
  • Content you choose to post in the Project Hub or Forum

If you use the hardware system connected to software, limited technical data may be collected to help improve performance and reliability.

How we use it

We use this information to:

  • Provide and maintain the NODE platform
  • Enable community features like posting and sharing projects
  • Improve the product and fix issues
  • Support learning experiences in educational settings

Sharing information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited data only when required to operate the service (for example, hosting or infrastructure providers) or to comply with legal obligations.

Children’s use

NODE is designed for educational use, including middle school students.

We aim to keep data collection minimal and appropriate for learning environments. We recommend that schools and educators supervise account creation where applicable.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit our Contact Us page.
